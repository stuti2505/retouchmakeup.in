WHAT IS HD BRIDAL MAKEUP?

HD MAkeup short stands for High Definition Makeup is a makeup technique and product category.
  • ITS primary purpose is to create a flawless,camera-ready complexion that looks natural and smooth
  • HD Makeup looks sheer while still hiding skin flaws like uneven texture ,pores , scars etc.
  • HD Makeup products typically have lightweight and breathable formulas.
  • It offers impart a matte or semi matte finish which reduces shine and gives the skin a velvety texture.

While HD makeup was originally intended for use in the entertainment industry,it has also become very popular in bridal makeup.It looks impeccable in photographs and videos and makeup artists often customise HD makeuo to suit the clients skin type and preferences

What is Airbrush Makeup?

Airbrush makeup is a technique that’s applied without brushes, sponges or fingers. Using a special sprayer, the machine creates an even surface of foundation across the skin. The system is created with a compressor that provides a smooth and controlled airflow through a medical-grade tube and nozzle. This apparatus is often used for theatre, film and bridal makeup.
